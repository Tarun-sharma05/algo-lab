## Concepts to learn first
- Declaration and initialization (int[], int[][])
- Traversal with for and for-each
- `Arrays.sort`, `Arrays.fill`, `Arrays.copyOf`, `Arrays.toString`
- Multi-dimensional arrays
- Time complexity: access O(1), search O(n), insert/delete O(n)
